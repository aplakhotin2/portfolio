Overview

ExpertBox is online meeting software that includes everything required for individual experts and agencies to provide the best online consultations to clients, from a seamless booking process and secure payment flow to high-quality video conferencing and instant chat messaging.

Challenges

Today, more and more companies and individuals in various domains, from healthcare to education to finance, are providing their services online. Online consultations offer benefits for companies and individual experts, permitting them to work remotely anytime and anywhere while allowing clients to access services fast and with little effort. This has led to growing demand for an all-in-one tool to deliver consultations online.

ExpertBox is a platform that provides companies and individuals with all the essential tools for holding online consultations. Our task was to combine HD video conferencing, convenient scheduling and client management, a secure payment system, and complex chat functionality into a single solution. To support all of this, we needed to build a clear and scalable architecture that would allow for the platform's growth.

Research
Carry out research and analysis to validate market demand.
Strategy
Clearly define a value proposition for the platform.
Platform
Create a fully functional online consulting platform covering video, scheduling, payments, and chat.
Scalability
Ensure scalability of the platform so it can broadcast a video meeting to as many as 100 participants in the future.

UI Design

For the website, Google Material Design UI framework was chosen for fast implementation and testing ideas in the most quick and appropriate way.

For mobile apps, an iOS app was designed natively, while for Android the decision was made to use the website design.

User Flow

User flows were created to figure out how real people will use the app — from the time they open it to the time they exit. User flows help determine the content and functionality that must be on a page. The main goal is to align user flows with users' specific goals.

Wireframes

Wireframes and prototypes visualize a user's interactions with an interface. This stage helps to review design solutions with project stakeholders, test them before implementation, and quickly make necessary amendments.

Design System

Design system is the single source of truth which groups all the elements - such as visual style, UI components, UX patterns, and branding style guide.
