About the Project

ExpertBox is an all-in-one cloud-based platform designed for service providers to manage appointments, conduct secure video consultations, automate administrative tasks, and handle billing — all within a single interface. It streamlines client interactions and business operations, making it ideal for professionals offering remote services across various industries.

My Role

As a Junior Product Designer at ExpertBox, I helped shape the product's design direction and user flows for key features such as dashboards, bookings, payments, and video calls. My work contributed to the scalability and consistency of the platform through design system development and close cross-functional collaboration.
