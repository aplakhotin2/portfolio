Overview

Designed a comprehensive diet app aimed at helping users achieve weight management goals and maintain a healthy lifestyle. Led the end-to-end UI/UX design process, from initial concept and extensive user research to creating a fully functional app with approximately 80 screens, tailored to diverse user needs.

The app gained recognition from a product manager in the USA and was successfully sold, enabling its transformation into a fully realized product.

Key Result

  • Designed ~80 screens covering the full user journey — onboarding, goal setting, meal tracking, workout planning, and progress analytics.
  • App was successfully sold to a US-based product manager and moved into full product development.
  • Built a cohesive brand identity from scratch, including color system, typography, and illustration style.

Challenge

The health and fitness app market is saturated with solutions that overwhelm users with features or oversimplify their needs. The challenge was to design an app that felt personal, motivating, and easy to use — while covering both nutrition tracking and fitness goals in a single coherent experience.

Starting from a blank canvas, I needed to validate the concept through user research, define the information architecture, and deliver a production-ready design across all core user flows.

Process

Research
Conducted user interviews and competitive analysis to identify pain points with existing fitness apps — including poor onboarding, lack of personalization, and unintuitive food logging.
Strategy
Defined a dual-focus concept — combining diet tracking and fitness planning under a unified goal-driven experience. Set core UX principles: simplicity, daily motivation, and visible progress.
Information Architecture
Mapped out all user flows and screen hierarchy. Prioritized onboarding to personalize the app from day one based on user goals, activity level, and dietary preferences.
UI Design
Designed ~80 screens across onboarding, dashboard, meal logging, workout plans, progress tracking, and settings. Created a custom design system with components, colors, and typography.
Branding
Developed the full brand identity — name, logo mark, color palette, and illustration style — to create a friendly and energetic visual language that appeals to a health-conscious audience.

Design Cycle

Key Screens

Onboarding
Personalized goal-setting flow that adapts the experience to each user's weight target, activity level, and food preferences from the very first launch.
Dashboard
Daily overview showing calorie balance, macro breakdown, water intake, and today's workout — all at a glance with clear visual progress indicators.
Meal Tracker
Quick food logging with barcode scanning, meal history, and nutritional breakdown. Designed for speed — logging a meal should take under 10 seconds.
Workout Planner
Guided workout sessions with exercise library, set/rep tracking, and rest timers. Integrates with the calorie counter to reflect burned calories in real time.
Progress
Weekly and monthly charts showing weight trend, calorie adherence, and workout consistency — motivating users to stay on track toward their goals.

Outcome

The project proved that a well-researched, polished design concept has standalone value. The app was recognized by a product manager based in the USA and successfully sold, allowing it to be developed into a real product.

The end-to-end ownership of this project — from user research through a complete 80-screen UI — sharpened my ability to design scalable mobile products and make independent product decisions.
