Overview

Metric Insights is the leading Business Intelligence Portal platform that helps organizations govern, organize, and deliver analytics at scale. Founded in 2012 and headquartered in San Francisco, it integrates all BI tools, reporting, and metrics into a single, consolidated catalog of governed and certified assets — making it simple for users to find, trust, and consume data.

My Role

At Metric Insights, my area of responsibility is leading design for the core platform and internal products. This includes the main BI Portal, homepage, search, data governance, and reporting interfaces used by 100K+ users daily.

Beyond design, I serve as Feature Owner for App Builder — a no-code platform that enables customers to create their own custom desktop apps. In this role I lead a team of two developers and two QA engineers, owning the full product lifecycle from discovery to delivery.

Core Problem

Organizations invest heavily in BI tools and analytics, yet employees struggle to find and trust the right data when they need it. Reports and dashboards are scattered across multiple platforms, creating confusion, duplication, and inconsistent metrics.

Users waste time searching, validating, and reconciling information instead of making decisions. As a result, valuable insights remain underutilized, and data-driven decision-making loses its impact.

Target Businesses & User Groups

Metric Insights is designed for medium-to-large enterprises that rely heavily on Business Intelligence and analytics but struggle with fragmented reporting tools, low BI adoption, and lack of trust in data. The platform provides a centralized BI Portal that integrates dashboards, reports, and metrics from BI tools into a single governed catalog.

BI Analysts
Create and maintain dashboards, reports, and metrics across different BI tools. They need a structured environment to organize and manage analytics content across teams.
IT & Data Teams
Manage BI infrastructure, integrations, and system security. They ensure reliable access to data and maintain the analytics environment.
Business Users
Managers and operational teams who rely on data for decision-making. They need quick access to relevant insights and trusted reports.
Data Governance Teams
Ensure that analytics content is accurate and consistent. They maintain data quality and trust across BI systems.

Process

At Metric Insights, our product design process began with prioritisation. The CEO and Product Owner collaborated with the Sales team to identify the next problem or feature to address. Once a priority was set, we initiated the discovery phase with the PM.

01 — Prioritisation
Discovery kick-off
CEO and Product Owner collaborated with Sales to identify the next problem or feature. Once prioritised, we initiated discovery with the PM — gathering data, understanding the problem's significance, and breaking down requirements.
02 — Stakeholders
Constant alignment
We maintained constant communication with various stakeholders — CEO, Product Owner, sales team, and developers. Daily communication with key members was crucial, along with weekly cross-team presentations to ensure alignment before implementation.
03 — Design
High-fidelity & iteration
After discovery, we moved into high-fidelity design — generating multiple options and refining them based on feedback. We tested all solutions with customers, subject matter experts, sales, and CX teams.
04 — Delivery
Implementation & follow-up
Close collaboration with engineering during implementation ensured designs were faithfully executed. Post-launch, we tracked usage metrics and gathered feedback to drive continuous improvement across the platform.

Challenges

Working on a complex enterprise platform over multiple years brought significant design and product challenges:

Outdated Design System
The existing design system contained outdated and inconsistent components. I redesigned and implemented a new unified design system across the entire MI platform, improving consistency and scalability.
Complex Data Analytics
Worked with complex data structures and analytics requirements to support seamless BI tool integrations across dozens of different platforms.
Speed vs. Quality
Balancing rapid delivery timelines — often within days or weeks — while maintaining a high-quality and consistent user experience across the platform.
BI Integration UX
Metric Insights integrates with a wide range of BI tools. Designing a consistent and intuitive UX across these diverse integrations was a significant ongoing challenge.
No-Code App Builder
Customer Apps could previously be built only through custom code. I designed a no-code App Builder and led its implementation — enabling self-serve app creation and reducing reliance on developers.
Enterprise Scale
Designing for companies ranging from 100 to 5,000 users with vastly different needs, data volumes, and governance requirements demanded scalable, flexible solutions.

Solution

Metric Insights is an enterprise Business Intelligence portal that centralizes analytics across multiple BI tools into a single, searchable hub. It allows organizations to discover trusted reports, dashboards, and metrics in one place, improving data accessibility and consistency across teams.

By providing a unified entry point to analytics, Metric Insights simplifies data governance, increases engagement with BI content, and helps companies make faster, more informed decisions based on reliable data.
